Sausage Rolls

Ingredients

1. 450g Sausages

2. 2 sheets of puff pastry
3. Finely chopped small onion
4. 2 cloves garlic
5. Salt & pepper to taste
6. 1 egg
Instructions for Cooking
1. Preheat the oven to 200 Celcius
2. In a bowl, mix the sausage with the finely chopped onions, garlic, salt & pepper
3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out each sheet of puff pastry
4. Divide the sausage meat mixture into two equal portions. Form each portion into a long sausage       shape & place it along one edge of each pastry sheet.
5. Roll the pastry
6. Cut into portions
7. Brush with egg wash
8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes
9. Get out the rolls from oven
10. Serve the Sausage rolls after few minutes
